📋 Copy-Paste Code Snippets

1. Facebook Page Plugin

<!-- Facebook Page Plugin – paste inside your page where you want the feed --> <iframe src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/page.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2FYOUR_PAGE&tabs=timeline&width=340&height=500&small_header=true&adapt_container_width=true&hide_cover=false&show_facepile=true" width="340" height="500" style="border:none;overflow:hidden;max-width:100%;" scrolling="no" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="true" allow="autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; picture-in-picture; web-share" loading="lazy" ></iframe> <!-- ⬆ Replace YOUR_PAGE with your Facebook page name (e.g. LockSafeUK) -->

2. Twitter / X Embedded Timeline

<!-- Twitter Timeline – paste where you want the feed --> <a class="twitter-timeline" data-height="500" data-theme="light" data-chrome="noheader nofooter noborders" href="https://twitter.com/YOUR_HANDLE"> Tweets by @YOUR_HANDLE </a> <script async src="https://platform.twitter.com/widgets.js"></script> <!-- ⬆ Replace YOUR_HANDLE with your X handle (e.g. LockSafeUK) --> <!-- Only include the script tag ONCE on your page -->

3. LinkedIn Follow Button

<!-- LinkedIn Follow Button – paste once in your page --> <script src="https://platform.linkedin.com/in.js" type="text/javascript">lang: en_US</script> <script type="IN/FollowCompany" data-id="YOUR_COMPANY_ID" data-counter="bottom"></script> <!-- ⬆ Replace YOUR_COMPANY_ID with your LinkedIn company page ID --> <!-- Find it at: linkedin.com/company/YOUR-SLUG → Admin → Settings --> <!-- For a FULL feed, use a third-party service like Elfsight or Curator.io --> <!-- Example (Elfsight): paste their provided script tag here -->
