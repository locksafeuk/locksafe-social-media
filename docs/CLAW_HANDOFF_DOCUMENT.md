## 2. LockSafe Business Overview

### What LockSafe Is
LockSafe is a **UK-wide, 24/7 emergency locksmith platform** that connects customers with verified locksmiths. It is NOT a locksmith company — it is a **technology platform** (marketplace) that acts as a trusted intermediary.

### Core Value Proposition
> *"The Only Platform That Prevents Price Scams"*

LockSafe's primary differentiator is a **legally-binding Digital Paper Trail** for every job:
- **GPS Tracking** — confirms locksmith arrival time and location
- **Timestamped Photos** — documents lock state, parts used, completed work
- **Digital Signatures** — captures customer approval at every stage
- **Instant PDF Reports** — consolidates all evidence into a legal document

### Two Target Audiences

| Audience | What They Want | Key Message |
|---|---|---|
| **Customers** | Emergency locksmith help without being scammed | "See the price before work begins. Full digital proof of everything." |
| **Locksmiths** | Guaranteed payment, verified customers, dispute protection | "Set your own fees. Get paid fairly. Your work is backed by evidence." |

## 6. Brand Voice & Messaging Guidelines

### Voice Characteristics
| Attribute | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Trustworthy** | Always emphasise transparency, verification, and proof | "Every job is documented with GPS, photos, and digital signatures" |
| **Protective** | Position LockSafe as a guardian against scams | "We built LockSafe because no one should be overcharged in an emergency" |
| **Professional** | Authoritative but approachable, never salesy or pushy | "Our locksmiths are DBS-checked, insured, and professionally qualified" |
| **Empathetic** | Acknowledge the stress of emergency situations | "Locked out at 2am? We know how stressful that is" |
| **Tech-Forward** | Highlight the digital paper trail as innovation | "Our legally-binding digital paper trail sets a new standard" |

### Messaging Do's and Don'ts

**✅ DO:**
- Lead with the problem (scams, overcharging, rogue traders) then present LockSafe as the solution
- Use specific numbers and facts (£25–£49 assessment fee, 15–30 min response, 4.9/5 rating)
- Reference the Digital Paper Trail in most posts
- Include the phone number (+44 20 4577 1989) and website (locksafe.uk) in CTAs
- Use the origin story (elderly parent overcharged) for emotional connection
- For recruitment: emphasise fair pay, guaranteed payment, professional credibility

**❌ DON'T:**
- Never badmouth specific competitors by name
- Never guarantee exact prices (always use ranges and "starting from")
- Never use aggressive sales language ("BUY NOW!", "LIMITED TIME!")
- Never post content that could be seen as fear-mongering
- Never claim LockSafe employs locksmiths (they are independent professionals on the platform)

### Hashtag Strategy
**Always use:** `#LockSafe` `#UKLocksmith`

**Rotate by category:**
- Home Security: `#HomeSecurity` `#PropertySecurity` `#HomeProtection` `#SecurityTips`
