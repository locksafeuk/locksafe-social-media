
# Locksafe Business Information

## Services Offered

Locksafe is a 24/7 emergency locksmith service platform operating across the UK. Their services are provided by a network of verified locksmiths. The platform covers a range of emergency and standard locksmith services, including:

- **Emergency Lockout Services:** For standard locks.
- **Lock Replacement:** Cylinder and mortice locks.
- **Lock Upgrades:** Including anti-snap locks.
- **uPVC Door Lock Repairs.**
- **Safe Opening.**

The platform's main feature is its anti-fraud system, which ensures transparent pricing and a legally-binding digital paper trail for every job.

## Geographic Coverage

- **UK-Wide:** Locksafe operates a nationwide network of locksmiths across the entire United Kingdom.
- **Response Time:** They advertise a 15-30 minute average response time.

## Branding and Messaging

- **Tone:** The tone is professional, reassuring, and focuses heavily on security, transparency, and customer protection.
- **Core Messaging:** "The Only Platform That Prevents Price Scams." This message is central to their brand and is repeated throughout their website. They emphasize that customers will see a full, legally-binding quote before any work begins.
- **Unique Selling Points (USPs):**
    - **Anti-Fraud Protection:** Their entire system is built to prevent scams and price gouging.
    - **Verified Locksmiths:** All locksmiths are DBS checked, insured, and professionally qualified.
    - **Transparent, Upfront Pricing:** Customers see an initial "assessment fee" and then a full "work quote" which they can accept or decline.
    - **Automatic Refund Guarantee:** If a locksmith is late, the customer receives a full refund.
    - **Legally-Binding Digital Paper Trail:** Every job is documented with GPS tracking, timestamped photos, and digital signatures, all compiled into a PDF report for the customer. This is a key differentiator.
    - **Customer Choice:** Customers can compare and choose from multiple locksmiths based on fees, ETA, and ratings.

## Existing Content and Resources

The website itself is the primary resource. It is well-structured to be a lead-generation and customer-assurance tool. Key content includes:

- **"How It Works" Section:** A step-by-step guide to using the platform.
- **Customer Testimonials:** Reviews from customers in London, Manchester, Birmingham, and Leeds.
- **FAQ Section:** Addresses common questions about the service, fees, and verification process.
- **Job Report Example:** A sample of the legally-binding digital report, which is a strong piece of content demonstrating their USP.
